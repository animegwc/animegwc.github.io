Kurozuka

12 Episodes

a named Minamoto no Yoshitsune (Kurou), flees into
the mountains after losing to his brother Minamoto no Yoritomo, the first
Shogun to rule all of Japan. History records that he committed suicide,
but instead, Kurou meets a strange, beautiful woman named Kuromitsu in her
mountain hermitage. Eventually, Kurou falls in love with Kuromitsu, but then
realizes she conceals a dark secret. He learns that he is unable to die and
continues to live for a thousand years as Japan evolves into a future society.


Season & Episode Episode Name Description
S1:E1 "The Plain of Adachigahara" Kuro and Benkei are on the run when they find a house in the middle of the mountains, owned by a beautiful and mysterious woman; Kuro falls ill and the two decide to stay the night at the house.
S1:E2 "The Tomb of Karma" Kuro drinks the blood of the immortal Kuromitsu, but while the power takes effect he is attacked by an evil enemy who has been stalking Kuromitsu for the secret behind her immortality.
S1:E3 "Asuka" Kuro awakes in the forest only to realize the entire world has changed and Karuta briefs him on how the world became post-apocalyptic due to the Great Disaster; Kuro attacks a Red Imperial Army hunting party and is severely wounded.
S1:E4 "Haniwa Man" Haunted by visions of a past he can't remember, Kuro searches on for his love; Rai brings Kuro to Izana who claims he will reunite the lovers if Kuro joins the Haniwa rebels against the Imperial Red Army.
S1:E5 "Saniwa" The Haniwa bring Kuro to meet the rebel leader who promises to reunite him with his lost love; the Red Army is desperate to find more of Kuromitsu's blood to breed a new army of enhanced soldiers.
S1:E6 "Miitsu" Arashiyama, the demonic General and his army close in on the Haniwa base in search of the Miitsu. Kuro and the Haniwa must stop at nothing to prevent the Red Army from acquiring the blood.
S1:E7 "Kagura Village" Kuro, Izana, and the other Haniwa journey to Kagura in search of Kuromitsu. Upon entering the underground dwelling of the residents they are shocked by what they find.
S1:E8 "The Ghost-Wheep Vine" In the forest of Kagura, Saniwa has fallen victim to the sorceress Kagetsu. Kuro must battle the vine-wielding sorceress and the winner will be closer to finding Kuromitsu.
S1:E9 "Running the Gauntlet" Kuro and the remaining Haniwa travel to the fortress of the Red Imperial Army in search of answers, but the road is fraught with death and danger.
S1:E10 "The Castle of Mirage" Kuro and Rai are captured in the fortress by Hasegawa. The truth of Kuro's dark past is revealed as is the true identity of Kuon.
S1:E11 "The Unending War" The truth is near but Kuro must protect Rai and defeat the mutated Hasegawa. Destroying him is only the beginning as he must confront Kuon who is desperate to eliminate any competition for Kuromitsu.
S1:E12 "The Black Tomb" Over 1000 years and Kuro returns to the same hut where his journey began. A battle for life, love, of master and apprentice is the key to the truth. This story has happened before and will happen again... for eternity.
