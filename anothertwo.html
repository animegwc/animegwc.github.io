Another

12 Episodes

5-year-old Kouichi Sakakibara transfers into class 3-3 of Yomiyama
North and soon after discovers that a strange, gloomy mood seems to hang over all the
students. He also finds himself drawn to the mysterious, eyepatch-wearing student Mei
Misaki; however, the rest of the class and the teachers seem to treat her like she doesn't
exist. Paying no heed to warnings from everyone including Mei herself, Kouichi begins to get
closer not only to her, but also to the truth behind the gruesome phenomenon plaguing class
3-3 of Yomiyama North.



Season & Episode Episode Name Description
S1:E1 "Rough Sketch" Koichi Sakakibara moves to his birth town of Yomiyama to live with his grandparents and his aunt Reiko Mikami, where he spends the first month hospitalized due to a pneumothorax. Once he recovers, he attends school, where Mei says that their class, Class 3-3, is close to death.
S1:E2 "Blueprint" At school, Koichi's classmate Naoya Teshigawara lets slip that Class 3-3 is cursed.
S1:E3 "Bone Work" Mei reveals that one of her eyes is a doll's eye that can see strange things. She tells Koichi the haunted legend of the town: a student from Class 3-3 of 1972 also named Misaki died but the class continued acting like Misaki was alive, thus inviting death.
S1:E4 "Put Flesh" Koichi encounters classmate Aya, whom he manages to save when they are nearly hit by a falling sheet of glass. At the doll shop, Mei tells Koichi that "it has already begun".
S1:E5 "Build Limbs" Ikuo Takabayashi dies next, and Koichi's classmates start ignoring him. Mei explains that she does exist. After the 1972 Class 3-3 incident, there was always an extra student in the class. The "extra" was dead but did not know it.
S1:E6 "Face to Face" Now that Koichi knows everything, he is okay with being ignored along with Mei, as Class 3-3 believes this "charm" will prevent the calamity. He and Mei begin spending time together and develop a good friendship.
S1:E7 "Sphere Joint" Shoji Kubodera, homeroom teacher of Class 3-3, commits suicide in front of the class. The class realizes that the "charm" has not stopped the curse, and begin acknowledging Koichi and Mei again.
S1:E8 "Hair Stand" They relax when they leave Yomiyama, convinced the curse is ineffective outside town. When Junta drowns and his body is torn up by a motorboat, everyone realizes that the curse may not be limited in Yomiyama.
S1:E9 "Body Paint" Chibiki reveals that Junta's death stemmed from a head trauma he received while still in Yomiyama. Koichi finds Katsumi's clue: a cassette tape, in which Katsumi explains what happened on the school trip to the Yomiyama Shrine fifteen years ago.
S1:E10 "Glass Eye" Class 3-3 goes on a trip to the Yomiyama Shrine, where the group listens to the repaired tape. Katsumi had accidentally killed one of the students, and when he discovered no one remembered the student, he realized he had killed the "extra", and thus stopped the curse.
S1:E11 "Makeup" Koichi finds that the manager has been killed, the dining hall is on fire, and the tape has gone missing. Naoya and Yuya are attacked by the hotel mistress, who has gone insane. Takako believes Mei is the "extra one", noticing a difference from the Misaki she knew in elementary, probably Fujioka.
S1:E12 "Stand by Oneself" Tomohiko is randomly killing students to find the "extra" but is killed by Izumi. Izumi is the next to die, in which she remembers meeting Koichi in Yomiyama in 1996. Koichi learns who the extra is.
